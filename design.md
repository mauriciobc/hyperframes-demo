## Overview
Loud, kinetic. Big product launches, high-energy hype videos. Text IS the visual. Overlapping type layers at different scales and angles. Bold saturated colors.

## Colors
Use `#0a0a0a` as the main background, `#ffffff` for contrast, and `#E63946` / `#FFD60A` for explosive color blocks and highlights.

## Typography
Use Google Fonts `Anton` for massive, chunky headlines, and `Space Grotesk` for technical, bold subheads.

## Motion
Everything kinetic: slamming, sliding, scaling. 2–3 second rapid-fire scenes. Fast arrivals, hard stops.
